Common Pitfalls to Avoid
Learn from others' mistakes

Financial Mistakes

‚ùå Spending Before Tax Planning
Many founders celebrate by immediately upgrading everything. Set aside taxes first, then decide on discretionary spending.
‚ùå Not Updating Projections
This payment changes your business model assumptions. Update your financial planning to match your new reality.
‚ùå Ignoring Cash Flow Timing
Large payments often come with long gaps. Don't assume this is your new normal cash flow.

Operational Mistakes

‚ùå Not Communicating with Your Accountant
Large payments can push you into new tax brackets or quarterly payment requirements. Get professional guidance.
‚ùå Poor Documentation
If the IRS comes knocking, or if there's ever a payment dispute, you'll need pristine records.
‚ùå Analysis Paralysis
Don't let the decision of "what to do with this money" paralyze your business operations. Make reasonable decisions and keep moving.
